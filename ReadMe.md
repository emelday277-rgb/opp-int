## About OppInt

OppInt is an intelligent assistant designed to help organizations quickly evaluate, prepare for, and win grant opportunities.

Instead of spending hours manually reviewing complex guidelines or struggling to organize your application documents, OppInt analyzes the requirements for you. It automatically checks if your organization is a good fit, identifies potential risks, and provides you with a clear, step-by-step roadmap to get your submission ready. By automating the heavy lifting, OppInt allows your team to focus on the quality of your project and increase your chances of securing funding.
