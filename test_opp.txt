GRANT OPPORTUNITY: Digital Innovation Fund 2024

PURPOSE:
The Digital Innovation Fund supports small and medium businesses developing 
technology solutions that improve operational efficiency or customer experience.
Grants of up to $50,000 USD are available.

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
- Business must be registered for at least 2 years
- Must have between 5 and 100 full-time employees
- Must be headquartered in an eligible region
- Must demonstrate previous technology project experience
- Annual revenue must not exceed $5 million USD

EVALUATION CRITERIA:
- Innovation and uniqueness of the proposed solution (30%)
- Technical feasibility and team capability (25%)
- Potential impact and scalability (25%)
- Budget justification and value for money (20%)

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Completed application form
- Business registration certificate
- Two-year financial statements
- Technical project proposal (max 10 pages)
- CVs of key team members
- Two reference letters from previous clients

DEADLINE:
Applications must be submitted by August 31, 2024.
Late submissions will not be considered.

SUBMISSION:
Submit all documents via the online portal at grants.digitalfund.org
All documents must be in PDF format.
